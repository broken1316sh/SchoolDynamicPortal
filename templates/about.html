Our History

Founded in 1995, Shree Gyan Bharti High School has been providing quality education to students in our community for over 25 years. We started with just 50 students and have grown to become one of the most respected institutions in the region.

Our Mission

To provide a nurturing environment that fosters academic excellence, character development, and lifelong learning. We aim to develop well-rounded individuals who will contribute positively to society.

Our Facilities

Our campus features modern classrooms, well-equipped science and computer labs, a spacious library, and excellent sports facilities to support holistic development of our students.
