Admission

Admission Information

Thank you for your interest in Shree Gyan Bharti High School. We strive to provide quality education and holistic development for all our students.

Please read through our admission process and requirements before submitting your application form.

Eligibility & Documents

  • Age Requirement: As per board norms
  • Birth Certificate: Original and photocopy
  • Transfer Certificate: From previous school (if applicable)
  • Report Card: From previous school
  • Passport Photos: 4 recent passport size photographs
  • Aadhar Card: Photocopy of student and parents
  • Address Proof: Any valid government document

Important Dates

  • Form Submission: Throughout the year
  • Entrance Test: As per schedule
  • Interview: After entrance test
  • Results: Within one week of interview
  • Fee Payment: Within 7 days of selection
