Admission Process

We invite you to join our vibrant learning community at {{ settings.school_name }}.

The admission process at our school is designed to understand the needs and aptitudes of prospective students, enabling us to provide them with the best educational experience. We welcome applications from students of all backgrounds who demonstrate a desire to learn and grow in our nurturing environment.

Admission Requirements
  • Completed application form
  • Previous academic records
  • Birth certificate
  • Passport-sized photographs
  • Character certificate from previous school (if applicable)
  • Transfer certificate from previous school (if applicable)
Selection Process

The selection process is based on the following criteria:

  1. Review of application and previous academic records
  2. Entrance assessment (for certain grades)
  3. Personal interview with the student and parents/guardians
  4. Availability of seats in the respective grade
Academic Year

The academic year at {{ settings.school_name }} begins in April and ends in March. Admissions for the new academic year typically open in January. However, mid-term admissions are considered subject to availability of seats.

Fee Structure

Our fee structure varies depending on the grade level. Please contact our administrative office for detailed information about the fees. The fee structure includes tuition fees, development fees, examination fees, and other miscellaneous charges.
